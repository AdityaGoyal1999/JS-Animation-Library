Wavy Text

The text appears normal until the mouse hovers over it and starts a wavy effect.

Devs can change background color, text color, bounce height and animation speed to suit their purpose.

Get hovering!

Backgroundy!

Implementation

JavaScript
addEventListener("DOMContentLoaded", ()=>{
const bg = new BackgroundGenerator();
bg.wavyText("#000", "#fff", 20, 1.25); };

You can alter the Wavy Text to suit the aesthetic of your website by passing the following required parameters -> (textColor, backgroundColor, pixelsBounceText, animationSpeed) to wavyText()

HTML

Note: The Red ids and classes are important in HTML for library to function.

	<div id="background-layer-1" class="sample-background">
		<div class="wavy-text-div">
			<h2 class="wavy-text">Backgroundy!</h2>
		</div>
	</div>

Eyes Move

The eyes of the background goes where ever the user takes the mouse. Also, the eyes blink!

Devs can change background color, eye color, eye lash color to suit their aesthetic purpose.

Get blinking!

Implementation

JavaScript
addEventListener("DOMContentLoaded", ()=>{
const bg = new BackgroundGenerator();
bg.eyesMotion("rgb(234, 195, 135)", "rgb(110, 72, 39)", "rgb(204, 175, 115)"); };

You can alter the colors of different parts of this background to suit the aesthetic of your website by passing the following required parameters -> (backgroundColor, eyeColor, eyeLashColor) to eyesMotion()

HTML

Note: The Red ids and classes are important in HTML for library to function.

	< id="background-layer-2" class="sample-background">
	</div>

Card Flip Animation

The card takes normal text and images and converts it into a card that can flip on hovering, making the space more efficient.

Devs can change card color, card width, card height to suit their aesthetic purpose.

Get Flipping!

Star Mouse Trail Effect

The mouse forms a trail of stars wherever you drag it on the designated area.

Devs can change star width, star height, background color and mouse trail length to suit their aesthetic purpose.

Keep Shining!

Implementation

JavaScript
addEventListener("DOMContentLoaded", ()=>{
const bg = new BackgroundGenerator();
bg.starMouseTrail("40", "40", "#000", 100);

You can alter the Mouse Trail to suit the aesthetic of your website by passing the following required parameters -> (starWidth, starHeight, backgroundColor, mouseTrailLength) to starMouseTrail()
